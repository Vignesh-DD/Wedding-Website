Haldi Ceremony

Wednesday 30 Oct 2024 ,11.30am Onwards

Venue :Home

The Haldi (turmeric) paste is believed to purify the bride and groom before their wedding, ensuring they are cleansed physically and spiritually. It is applied to the skin as a way to ward off evil spirits and negative energy, providing protection during this sacred time.

Mehendi Ceremony

Friday 01 Nov 2024 ,3.00pm Onwards

Venue :Home

Mehendi (henna) is considered a symbol of prosperity and good fortune in Indian culture. Applying it to the bride’s hands and feet is believed to bring blessings, happiness, and good luck for her marital journey.he darker the bride’s Mehendi stain, the more it is believed that her husband and in-laws will love her.

Ring Ceremony & Reception

Saturday 02 Nov 2024 ,7.00pm Onwards

Venue :Wedding hall

The couple exchanges rings as a symbol of their commitment and promise to each other. It’s a significant moment that reflects the beginning of their journey toward marriage.A pre-wedding reception is held to formally introduce the bride or groom to the extended family and friends. This event creates a celebratory atmosphere where everyone can meet and bond before the big day.

Mangalya Dharanam

Sunday 03 Nov 2024 ,10.00am Onwards

Venue :Wedding hall

The Mangalsutra is a symbol of the marital bond, representing the lifelong commitment between the couple. The act of tying it during the wedding ceremony signifies that the bride and groom are now officially husband and wife.
